## Skill Coverage Gaps (Tracked)

These skill areas have been identified as missing or under-documented:

- [ ] **Reasoning**: Probabilistic reasoning, Bayesian inference
- [ ] **Memory**: Hierarchical memory management, cache eviction strategies
- [ ] **Code**: IDE integration, pair-programming patterns
- [ ] **Security**: Red-teaming, jailbreak resistance, adversarial robustness
- [ ] **Domain-Specific**: Healthcare NLP, legal document analysis, scientific literature review
- [ ] **Orchestration**: Multi-modal pipelines, cross-agent authentication

> Open an issue or PR to claim a gap item.

---

## How to Propose a Roadmap Item

1. Open a [Discussion](https://github.com/SamoTech/skills-tree/discussions) with the tag `roadmap`.
2. Describe the item, its value, and its scope.
3. If accepted, it will be added here with ⚪ status.
4. Assign yourself and submit a PR when ready.
